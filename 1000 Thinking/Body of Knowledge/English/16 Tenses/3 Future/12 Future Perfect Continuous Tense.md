> Berfungsi untuk menjabarkan

>[!Tip]
>Formula = **S + Will + Have + Been + Verb -ing (Continuous Form)**

---
Example
---

1. She will have been sleeping long when you get home.
2. He will have been taking charge of the team for the next hour.
