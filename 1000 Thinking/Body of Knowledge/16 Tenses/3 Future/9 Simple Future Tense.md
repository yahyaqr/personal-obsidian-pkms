>[!One Sentence summary]
> Berfungsi untuk menjabarkan

>[!Tip]
>Formula = **S + Will + Verb 1 (Present Form)**

>[!Tip]
>Formula = **S + Is/Are/Am + going to + Verb 1 (Present Form)**

---
Example
---

1. She is going to be a writer.
2. He will win the game.
